# Josh's Tea Journal Archive

This is an archived version of Josh's tea blog originally hosted at [fishkarmatea.blogspot.com](https://fishkarmatea.blogspot.com/), documenting tea reviews from London between December 2009 and September 2011.

## About the Blog

Josh's Tea Journal features personal tasting notes and reviews of various teas, primarily from London tea shops including:
- Whittards
- Fortnum & Mason
- Terra Nera Tea Boutique (Camden Market)

The blog covers a wide range of teas including:
- Assam varieties
- Darjeeling (first and second flush)
- Ceylon teas
- Blends
- Flavored teas (Mango, Passion Fruit, Arabian, Blue Lady)
- Chinese black teas

## Rating System

Teas are rated on a 10-point scale based on "price and overall taste," with ratings ranging from 4/10 to 8/10 across the archived posts.

## Archive Contents

- **18 blog posts** spanning from December 5, 2009 to September 14, 2011
- All posts preserved in markdown format with complete metadata
- Original URLs preserved for reference
- Posts organized chronologically in the `/posts` directory
