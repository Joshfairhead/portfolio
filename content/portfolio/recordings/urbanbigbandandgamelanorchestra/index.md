# Urban Big Band – Notes from rehearsal 6th March 2012
• 4 x Trumpets – normally left stage
• 4 x Trombones (1 bass maybe) – normally centre stage
• 5 x Saxophones (1 baritone, 2 tenor & 2 alto doubling on woodwind)
Baritone Saxophone (doubles on Clarinet, Bass Clarinet, Flute)
Tenor Saxophone 2 (doubles on Clarinet, Flute, Piccolo)
Alto Saxophone 1 & 2 (double on Clarinet) normally all right stage
• Piano: electric Roland RD700, mono feed normally back-left stage
• Bass: electric normally back-right stage
• Drums: kick, snare, OH normally back-centre stage
